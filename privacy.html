Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Baldwin Tree LLC handles information for Lightning Numerology. This policy is intended to cover the app on Apple App Store platforms and, if released there, Google Play platforms.

Summary

Lightning Numerology is a quiz game. The app does not require a custom app account and does not ask for a custom player name. If you choose to use the public leaderboard, the app uses Apple Game Center rather than a developer-operated score backend. Skin unlock progress is stored locally on your device.

Information We Store Locally

The app stores limited unlock progress locally on your device, including unlocked skin IDs, current unlock streak progress, current unlock games progress, best streak, and total games played. This progress is used only to unlock optional visual skins inside the app.

Local unlock progress is not transmitted to the developer and is not sold. You can remove this local data by deleting the app from your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

The app itself does not collect or request:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account credentials
  • Camera, microphone, contacts, health, or precise location access
  • Payment information
  • User-generated public content
  • Custom app-hosted leaderboard profiles or online score records

Game Center Leaderboard

The app may offer an optional public leaderboard through Apple Game Center. If you are signed in to Game Center and use leaderboard features, the app may submit your final game score to Apple Game Center after Game Over. Game Center may display your Game Center nickname, avatar, and score to other Game Center users according to Apple's Game Center and Apple ID privacy terms.

The developer does not operate a separate leaderboard server, does not collect custom leaderboard names, and does not provide public comments, chat, or user-generated leaderboard text. Local unlock progress remains stored on your device separately from Game Center.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to show third-party banner ads and interstitial ads after Game Over. The app does not include rewarded ads. Users may make an optional one-time in-app purchase to remove banner and interstitial ads.

Ads are configured for non-personalized/contextual ad serving, and Google's publisher first-party identifier is disabled. The app does not request Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission and does not intentionally request access to the IDFA. Before requesting ads, the app uses Google's User Messaging Platform to request and record consent choices where required. A Privacy Choices control is made available in the app when Google indicates that users must be able to revisit those choices. Active timed gameplay is kept ad-free.

Even when non-personalized ads are used, Google may process information to deliver ads, limit fraud and abuse, cap ad frequency, measure ad performance, and comply with legal requirements. Depending on device, region, consent choices, and Google SDK behavior, this may include coarse location inferred from an IP address, device identifiers, advertising data, product interaction, crash data, performance data, other diagnostic data, IP address, and app or device information. Some data may be linked to a device or used by Google for advertising or measurement purposes. Google handles this information according to its own policies.

Learn more about Google privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy and Google advertising technologies at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

In-App Purchases

The optional Remove Ads purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive or store your payment card or bank information. The app uses Apple-provided transaction and entitlement information only to complete, restore, and maintain access to the purchased ad-free feature. Apple's handling of purchase information is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Consent and Tracking

The app is configured for non-personalized ads and does not include an App Tracking Transparency prompt or intentionally access the IDFA. Google may nevertheless use device, advertising, interaction, or technical data as described above, subject to consent choices and Google's own definitions and practices. The app requests consent information before loading ads and does not load ads when Google's SDK reports that ads cannot be requested. If advertising or platform requirements change, this policy and the app's store disclosures will be updated.

Analytics

The app does not include a separate developer analytics SDK. The developer may receive aggregated information made available by app stores or ad platforms, such as basic download, crash, or ad performance reporting, subject to those platforms' policies.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can address the issue.

Data Sharing

The developer does not sell your personal information. The app shares data only as necessary for integrated third-party advertising through Google AdMob, as described above, or as required by law.

Data Retention

Local unlock progress remains on your device until you delete the app. Third-party advertising data is retained by Google according to Google's own policies.

Your Choices

  • You can delete local unlock progress by deleting the app from your device.
  • You can make an optional one-time purchase to remove ads and restore that purchase through the app.
  • You can manage Game Center participation through your Apple ID and device Game Center settings.
  • You can manage device-level ad and privacy settings through your operating system settings.
  • You can review Google's privacy controls through Google's privacy resources linked above.

Security

The app is designed to minimize data collection. Local unlock progress is stored using standard device storage. No method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but the app avoids collecting account credentials or sensitive personal information.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When changes are made, the effective date above will be updated. Continued use of the app after an updated policy is posted means you accept the updated policy.
