The Numerology Game Support

Help with gameplay, ads, purchases, audio, and local game history.

Contact Support

Email baldtreesupport@protonmail.com. Please include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.

Remove Ads Purchase

Remove Ads is a one-time, non-consumable purchase. If you previously purchased it, open the Remove Ads page from the dollar-sign button on the start screen and select Restore Purchases. Use the same Apple Account that made the original purchase.

Ads

The app may display non-personalized banner ads on the Menu and Game Over screens, plus interstitial ads after Game Over. Ads do not appear during active timed gameplay. A verified Remove Ads purchase disables both ad formats.

Audio

The music button controls background music and correct or incorrect answer sounds together. Also confirm that your device volume is raised and Silent Mode is configured as desired.

Game History

The latest 35 games are stored locally on your device. The app does not use an online leaderboard or account. Deleting the app also deletes its locally stored history.

Privacy

Review the Privacy Policy for details about local storage, advertising, and the optional Remove Ads purchase.
